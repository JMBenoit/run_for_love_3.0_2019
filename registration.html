Details

The Run For Love 5K is an annual event to help raise money to fight substance use and addiction.

  • Held rain or shine
  • Walk or run - feel free to go at your own pace
  • Dogs are welcome
  • There will be a raffle booth and tickets sold at the event
  • Water will be provided at the start and finish, and at the halfway point.
  • Option to be timed with prizes for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd.

Registration

Registration is $25 if purchased online before May 31st. Otherwise, it is $30 and can be bought upon entry to the 5K.

With your registration, you will receive:

  • 1x Run For Love 5K T-shirt (If registered online before May 31st)
  • 1x Raffle ticket
  • Lunch
  • View performance of the Plymouth, NH chear team
  • Listen to multiple stories from people who have faced and overcome substance abuse and/or addiction
  • Fight the stigma of substance abuse
  • Help those fighting addiction
