Sponsorships

Thank you for your interest in sponsoring the Run For Love 5K. Your sponsorship helps with the cost of organizing an event like this one. We use our sponsorship funds to help with the costs of t-shirts, prizes, raffle items, print materials, drinks, and food. All extra money goes towards our fundraising for Father Bills & Mainspring. There are a couple different packages available, which are listed below.

If you are interested in purchasing a sponsorship package, please email us at runforlove5kma@gmail.com, or use the contact form at the bottom of the page.

Platinum - $1000

  • Title sponsorship - "Run For Love 5K powered by ("Your Company")
  • Prominant recongition on all race advertisements
  • 10' x 10' booth space at event
  • Signage along race route
  • Extra large name or logo on the front of the race t-shirts
  • Top recognition on website under current sponsors with linked logo
  • 5 complimentary race entries

Gold - $500

  • 10' x 10' booth space at event
  • Signage along race route
  • Name or logo on the back of the race t-shirts
  • Recognition on website under current sponsors with linked logo
  • 3 complimentary race entries

Silver - $250

  • Signage along race route
  • Name or logo on the back of the race t-shirts
  • Recognition on website under current sponsors with linked logo
  • 2 complimentary race entries

Bronze - $100

  • Name or logo on the back of the race t-shirts
  • Recognition on website under current sponsors with linked logo
  • 1 complimentary race entry

In-Kind

  • Provide food, drink, or raffle item donation
  • Recongnition on website under current sponsors with linked logo
